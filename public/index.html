ABOUT ME

Front End Developer

I’m a web developer and Graphic Designer looking for work that will utilise the skills I have aquired. Most recently, I was employed at the Police National Headquarters, analysing software and its effectiveness to serve both the police and our clients.It was here that a flame was reignited around creative processes and solutions that really serve the user. I have found myself gravitating towards front end roles in a team environment because of my background in Graphic Design. I also got to see another side of myself by pushing beyond my limits and to also see how others found working with me. I am a team orientated person who is well spoken energetic, confident and personable. I wanted to build and create more than just assets but able to control and understand the software that I was using to implement those properties. Much to the dismay of my previous employers, I took the leap and found that studying Web Development at Enspiral Dev Academy was the best solution. I enjoy working with people and have had a wide experience working in other countries (including non-english speaking countries) I’m hoping that with the skills I have obtained will provide me with an interactive learning experience that is challenging and self enhancing.

My Name


Born and raised in the small town of Whangarei where I lived and breathed the outdoors and had a fascination for playing sports. After highschool, I attended Auckland University of Technology where I was planning to study Sports and Recreation. From here, I realized my full potential in design and felt a pull into the world of art. So I over the years I studied Art and Design before obtaining a degree from Raffles School of Design and Commerce. After I graduated, I embarked on my Overseas experience where I lived and worked in South Korea for four years, understanding and learning the ways of different culture was such a surrealist opportunity for me. Something I will cherrish for the rest of my life. I then went on to work and live in the United States for one year before finally returning to New Zealand where I studied Web Development and Enspiral Dev Academy.
